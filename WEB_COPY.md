### 2.2 The problem

**Headline**
> Running has been hijacked by metrics.

**Body**
> Open any popular running app and the numbers come at you mid-stride —
> live pace, zone alarms, cadence prompts, segment leaderboards, the
> unspoken pressure to perform for an audience while your lungs are still
> warming up.
>
> Easy runs get pushed too fast because the pace is being watched.
> Recovery jogs become zone-2 audits. The last 200 metres turn into a
> sprint to fix an average. The run we set out for — the one that was
> supposed to feel good — quietly disappears under the dashboard.
>
> **What started as tools to help runners have become tools that own them.**
> The run worth having, it turns out, is the one you stayed present in —
> and the one you can still remember a year later.

### 3.2 Pillar — Show up.

**Eyebrow** — THE PATH

**Lead**
> The act. The rhythm. The shoes-on, out-the-door, log-it-when-you're-back
> side of running. ZenRun stays quiet so you can stay present — your watch,
> your phone, and your breath, all working together to make the run easy
> to start.

**3.2.1 Run Logging** — _Run first. Log later. Get on with your day._
- ⏱️ **2-Second Logging** — Pick your distance, enter your time, done. Use your Apple Watch or not. Run however you want, log it when you're back.
- 🏃 **9 Distances** — 1K through 21K — including 1K, 2K, 3K, 5K, 8K, 10K, 15K, 18K, and 21K. Outdoor or treadmill. Pick the one that fits your day.
- 📝 **Backdate & Edit** — Forgot to log? Add runs from previous days. Made a mistake? Edit distance, time, or category anytime. Your log, your rules.

**3.2.2 Apple Watch Companion** — _A quiet wrist. GPS captured in the background._
- ⌚ **One-Tap Start** — Open the watch app, pick walk or run, tap once. ZenRun records the route, syncs to your phone when you're back, and gets out of the way.
- 🛰️ **Background GPS** — Your route is traced and saved without you having to look at your wrist. The line of where you went is yours forever.
- 🔋 **A quiet companion** — Distance, time, heart rate captured calmly on your wrist. The data lands in the journal afterwards, ready when you are.

**3.2.3 Walks** — _Walks are first-class. Show up however you can._
- 🚶 **Walks as Real Activities** — Same logging flow. Same scenic photos. Same maps and recaps. Walks count toward your rhythm, your goals, and your story.
- ☔ **On the Bad-Weather Days** — When a run isn't happening, a walk still is. ZenRun protects your rhythm by treating both with equal respect.

**3.2.4 Rhythm & Goals** — _We reward showing up, not showing off._
- 🌳 **Weekly Rhythm** — Run or walk at least twice a week to keep your rhythm going. Not pace. Not distance. Just presence. Miss a week? It's a pause — you come back.
- 🎯 **Monthly & Yearly Goals** — Set your own km targets. A simple progress bar shows where you are and where you should be. No judgement.
- 🎉 **Quiet Celebrations** — A small moment of confetti when you maintain your rhythm or hit a goal. No fanfare. Just a nod.

- 🖼️ **Scenic Runs Gallery** — Browse all your scenic runs in one beautiful album. Full-screen photos, captions, and a timeline of each run's journey.

**3.3.2 Maps & Routes** — _Every line you traced, kept._
- 🛣️ **Run & Walk Maps** — Every GPS-tracked activity gets a map you can revisit. The shape of your morning loop. The unfamiliar park you wandered through. The city you ran in on holiday.
- 📐 **Distance, Time, Elevation** — The fundamentals, recorded automatically. Pace per km when you want it. Elevation gain for the hill days.

- 🔥 **Active Energy** — Calories burned during your run, recorded by HealthKit. Available when you want it, never shouted at you.

**3.3.4 Recaps & Records** — _Your journey, marked the way you want to remember it._

- ⚡ **Personal Records** — Your fastest time at each distance, tracked automatically. Filter by outdoor or treadmill. The opponent is yourself, the way you used to be.
- 🏆 **100 Milestones** — Across distance, first completions, rhythm, and goals. Each badge celebrates a marker in your own journey.

**Hero**

**FAQ — Getting Started**
- _How do I log a run?_ → Tap the Run tab, pick your distance (1K to 21K), choose outdoor or treadmill, enter your time, and tap Save. The whole thing takes about 2 seconds.
- _What distances can I log?_ → 9 distances: 1K, 2K, 3K, 5K, 8K, 10K, 15K, 18K, and 21K. All available at every runner level.
- _Can I backdate a run I forgot to log?_ → Yes. When logging a run, tap the date field to pick any past date. You can also edit or delete any run from your history.
- _Does ZenRun use GPS?_ → No. ZenRun deliberately does not track your location. Run without your phone if you want. Log it when you're back.
  - **(Stale: GPS now exists for outdoor runs and walks. Rewrite.)**

**FAQ — Rhythm & Milestones**
- _How does rhythm work?_ → Run at least twice in a calendar week (Mon–Sun) and your rhythm continues. Miss a week and it pauses — but your longest rhythm is always saved. It's not a streak that punishes you. It's a rhythm you come back to.
- _What is the rhythm plant?_ → Your rhythm is visualised as a growing plant — from a seed (🌰) at week 0 to a mighty oak (🌲) at 26+ weeks. It grows with your consistency.
- _How many milestones are there?_ → 100 milestones across 8 categories: run count, total distance, first completions, rhythm, goals, scenic runs, speed, and special achievements.
- _What are runner levels?_ → You progress through four levels — Breath, Stride, Flow, and Zen — based on your total runs. Each level represents a deeper stage of your running journey.

**FAQ — Circles & Community**
- _What are circles?_ → Private groups of up to 10 friends. You see each other's runs, react to activity, and share scenic photos — accountability without competition.
- _How do I create or join a circle?_ → Go to the Circles tab. To create, tap "Create Circle" and share the invite code. To join, tap "Join" and enter the code your friend shared.
- _What are web profiles?_ → You can share your running journey on the web at zenrun.co/runner/your-handle. Choose your privacy: fully private, visible to circles only, or public for everyone to see.
- _How do I set my profile visibility?_ → Go to your Profile screen in the app and choose from Private, Circles, or Public under Profile Visibility. You can change it anytime.

**FAQ — Account & Privacy**
- _Can I change my handle?_ → No. Handles are permanent once set. Choose carefully when you create yours in the Profile screen.
- _I forgot my password. How do I reset it?_ → Tap "Forgot password?" on the login screen, enter your email, and we'll send you a 6-digit reset code. Enter the code and your new password to get back in.
- _How do I delete my account?_ → Email support@zenrun.co from your registered email address and we'll delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
- _Is ZenRun free?_ → Yes. ZenRun is completely free. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no ads.
- _What data does ZenRun collect?_ → Only what you explicitly log: runs, times, scenic photos, step days, and weight (if you choose to track it). We don't track location, browsing, or any background data.
  - **(Stale: location is collected for outdoor GPS-tracked runs/walks when the user starts one. Rewrite.)**

**Response time card**
> We typically respond within **48 hours**.
